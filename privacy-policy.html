Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Tony's Tools ("we", "us", or "our") handles your data when you use our tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Data Processing

All tools in this collection process data exclusively in your browser. No personal data is collected or transmitted to any server unless explicitly stated otherwise for specific tools.

Client-Side Processing

Our tools are designed to run entirely in your browser. This means:

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Your data remains under your control

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data through our tools. However, when you visit our website, GitHub Pages (our hosting provider) may collect certain technical information such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Time and date of access

GitHub Pages

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's privacy policy for information about how they handle data: GitHub Privacy Statement

External Links

Our tools may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please use the GitHub Issues page:
